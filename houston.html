City Overview

Houston is the most populous city in Texas. With a population of 2,302,878 in 2022, Houston is the fourth-most populous city in the United States. Houston was founded by land investors on August 30, 1836. Houston has become a global city, with strengths in culture, medicine, and research. Houston is the most diverse metropolitan area in Texas and has been described as the most racially and ethnically diverse major city in the U.S.

(Source: Wikipedia)
