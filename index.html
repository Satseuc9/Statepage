The Great State of Texas

Texas is the second-largest state in the United States, both by area and population. It covers an area of about 268,596 square miles

The capital of Texas is Austin. It is known for its vibrant music scene, cultural events, and being the home of the University of Texas.

Texas is famous for its cattle ranches and oil production. The state leads the nation in both cattle and oil production.
