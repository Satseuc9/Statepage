City Overview

Austin is the capital of the U.S. state of Texas. Incorporated on December 27, 1839. It is the tenth-most populous city in the United States. The urban city's official slogan promotes Austin as "The Live Music Capital of the World". Located in Central Texas within the greater Texas Hill Country, it is home to numerous lakes, rivers, and waterways.

(Source: Wikipedia)

Region

Austin, city, capital of Texas, U.S., and seat (1840) of Travis county. It is located where the Colorado River crosses the Balcones Escarpment in the south-central part of the state. It is located about 80 miles northeast of San Antonio.

(Source: Britannica)
